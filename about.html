Our Story

TopShield Roofing has been serving the Philadelphia area for over 15 years, providing exceptional roofing services to homeowners and property owners throughout Pennsylvania.

What started as a small family-owned business has grown into one of the most trusted roofing contractors in the region. Our commitment to quality workmanship, honest pricing, and customer satisfaction has earned us hundreds of five-star reviews and countless referrals.

We treat every roof as if it were our own, ensuring that each project is completed to the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
