Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our roofing services

The cost varies depending on the size of your roof, materials chosen, and the extent of damage. Minor repairs can range from $300-$1,500, while full roof replacements typically range from $8,000-$25,000. We offer free estimates to give you an accurate quote for your specific project.
Yes! We have extensive experience working with insurance companies for storm damage and other covered events. We can help document damage, provide detailed estimates, and work directly with your insurance adjuster to ensure a smooth claims process.
Most residential roof replacements take 1-3 days to complete, depending on the size and complexity of the roof. Weather conditions and material availability can affect timing. We'll provide you with a clear timeline during your estimate and keep you informed throughout the project.
We work with a variety of quality roofing materials including asphalt shingles (architectural and 3-tab), metal roofing, slate, tile, and flat roofing systems. We'll help you choose the best material for your home based on your budget, style preferences, and durability requirements.
We offer comprehensive warranties on both materials and labor. Material warranties range from 25 years to lifetime depending on the product, while our workmanship warranty covers labor for up to 10 years. We'll provide full warranty details with your estimate.
Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency roofing services for urgent situations like severe leaks, storm damage, or structural issues. Call us anytime at 215-594-5828, and we'll dispatch a team to assess and address the emergency as quickly as possible.
We recommend having your roof inspected at least once a year, preferably in spring or fall. Additionally, you should have an inspection after any major storm or weather event. Regular inspections help identify minor issues before they become major problems.
Absolutely! TopShield Roofing is fully licensed and carries comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. This protects you and your property throughout the entire project. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.
The lifespan of your roof depends on the materials used and maintenance. Asphalt shingles typically last 20-30 years, metal roofs can last 40-70 years, and slate or tile roofs can last 50-100+ years. Proper installation and regular maintenance are key to maximizing your roof's lifespan.
Common signs include missing or damaged shingles, curling or buckling shingles, granules in gutters, water stains on ceilings, daylight through the roof boards, a sagging roofline, or a roof that's 20+ years old. If you notice any of these signs, contact us for a free inspection.
Yes! We work with major roofing manufacturers who offer a wide range of colors and styles. We can help you match your existing roof or choose a new color that complements your home's exterior. We'll bring samples during your consultation.
It's not necessary to be home the entire time, but we do recommend being available at the start and end of each day. This allows us to walk you through our progress, address any questions, and ensure you're satisfied with the work being done.
We take several precautions including covering landscaping and outdoor items, using tarps to protect siding and windows, placing magnetic sweepers to pick up nails, and conducting thorough cleanup at the end of each day. Your property protection is a top priority.
We accept cash, checks, all major credit cards, and offer financing options for qualified customers. Typically, we require a deposit to schedule the work and final payment upon completion. We'll discuss payment terms in detail when providing your estimate.
